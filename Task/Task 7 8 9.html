Nature

The second aspect of beauty Emerson considers is the spiritual element. Great actions in history are often accompanied by a beautiful backdrop provided by nature.
The third aspect in which nature should be viewed is its value to the human intellect.
Nature can help to inspire people to create and invent new things. Everything in nature is a representation of a universal harmony and perfection, something greater than itself.

'Nature' is an 1836 essay by the American writer and thinker Ralph Waldo Emerson (1803-82)
